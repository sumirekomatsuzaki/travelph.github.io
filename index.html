Everything I Did

The original website design was a generic template for a frozen yogurt shop, featuring placeholders for product descriptions, a blog, and contact details. I changed it to a travel-focused website, Explore Philippines, emphasizing famous holiday destinations and cultural experiences in the Philippines. Key changes included replacing all yogurt-related images, text, and links with content highlighting popular Philippine destinations, such as Boracay, the Puerto Princesa Underground River, and the Chocolate Hills. The HTML was updated to reflect the travel theme, altering section headers, image descriptions, and navigation links to align with the topic. CSS adjustments ensured a cohesive color scheme and typography suitable for a travel website, enhancing the visual appeal and readability while maintaining the responsive layout of the original design.
